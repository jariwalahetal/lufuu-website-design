CHARITIES

GET AWARENESS

By posting about their mission, charities can bring awareness about their organization to other users. Users can follow the charities progress, view any announcements, and donate from their Lufuu bank.

BUILDING A RELATIONSHIP WITH USERS

This is an opportunity to interact and get users interested in your charity's work. By following your charity's profile, the user learns more about your charity and feels like they are contributing and making a positive difference. As well, you benefit from gaining new supporters and accomplish many projects from the donations.

AVAILABLE TO ALL

Charities in Lufuu will fall under twelve categories:

  • Animal & Animal Protection
  • Arts, Culture, and Humanities
  • Children
  • Crime Prevention
  • Education
  • Environment
  • Health
  • Homelessness & Housing
  • Human Rights & Services
  • Hunger
  • International Relief and Development
  • Religion
