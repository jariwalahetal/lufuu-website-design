ABOUT LUFUU

LUFU - OLD ENGLISH FOR LOVE

Once the power of love overcomes the love of power, the world will know peace.

Lufuu is a social media application that facilitates charitable giving to make a positive difference in the world.

Lufuu allows users to donate to their favorite charities by watching ads catered to their interests. Following the ad, users must answer a couple of brief questions correctly to receive monetary credits, allowing them to donate to the charities.

GIVING BACK EASIER FOR ALL

We understand that not everyone can afford to donate to charity, or wish that they can donate more.
With Lufuu, we are re-thinking the traditional donation method and making giving back easier. Read More

Introduction

Our Strength

WANTING TO DO MORE?

Have you ever wanted to make a change, to move the world in a positive direction?
Lufuu proves that if many people come together, we can push and drive the world in a better direction.

Founded in May 2017 by two sisters from Montreal, Lufuu provides a tool that can help so many. It is for good reason that this app is called Lufuu; it means love in old English. And that is what we are trying to spread. Love.

Thank you for giving Lufuu a chance, and for affecting change. Give a little Love, give a little Lufuu.
